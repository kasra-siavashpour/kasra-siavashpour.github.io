I'm a final-year Computer Science student at Sharif University of Technology, one of Iran's most prestigious institutions. My research focuses on networking and distributed systems, with particular emphasis on IoT networks, autonomous aerial/vehicular systems (FANET/VANET), and cluster-based routing optimization using SDN and machine learning techniques. I've had the privilege of collaborating with research groups at Sharif, Institute for Research in Fundamental Sciences (IPM), and Politecnico di Milano, working on cutting-edge solutions for modern network infrastructures and decentralized cooperative systems.
