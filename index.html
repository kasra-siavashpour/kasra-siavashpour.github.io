About Me

Sharif University of Technology

B.Sc. Computer Science | 2020–2025

I'm a final-year Computer Science student at Sharif University of Technology, passionate about solving complex networking challenges in IoT, autonomous systems, and distributed computing. My research focuses on energy-efficient routing protocols, cluster-based optimization, and systems performance engineering. I've had the privilege of working with research groups at Sharif and Politecnico di Milano, exploring cutting-edge solutions for modern network infrastructures.
